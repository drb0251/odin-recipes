How to make a delicious burger

Beef burger with fries

This burger recipe is perfect for those hot summer evenings, watching the sunset while you sink your teeth into the juicy premium mince patty, chomp down some crunchy fries, and drown it all down with a nice cold tap beer. On ya mate!

Or you could just stay home watching Netflix, here's the ingredient list anyways!

  • Premium mince 500g
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Gherkins
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Sesame buns
  • Fries

Now here's the recipe instructions. Note, you need to follow this thing very precisely! (lol)

  1. Slice tomato and gherkins
  2. Wash the lettuce
  3. Finely slice the cheese
  4. Fry the patties on the grill
  5. Assemble the burger and squirt your fav sauce on that bad boy
  6. Fry the fries
  7. Pour a beer
  8. Voila
