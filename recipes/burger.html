How to make a great steak

Delicious mushroom sauce steak

Take a look at this amazing steak! Packed with 300g of lean protein and a bit of asparagus and potato - not too many veges, we don't want to get sick!

All jokes aside here's the ingredient list!

  • Prime Angus Steak 500g
  • 200g Asparagus (like 3 pieces)
  • baby potatos (approx 250g)
  • garlic & herb spice mix
  • salt & pepper

Now here's the recipe instructions. Note, you need to follow this thing very precisely! (lol)

  1. Cook the steak on your grill at 200 degrees for 10 minutes
  2. Cook your baby potatoes at 200 degrees in your airfryer with some oil and garlic and herb mix
  3. Fry the asaparagus in a pan set to high heat
  4. Plate up and add salt and pepper to taste
